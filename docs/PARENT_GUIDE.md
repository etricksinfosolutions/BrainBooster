# Parent Guide 👨‍👩‍👧

## Getting started
Open the app, tap **Play** — that's it. Your child's progress saves automatically on the device, even offline.

## The Parent Dashboard
From the home screen tap **Parents**. You'll answer a simple multiplication question (this keeps children out).

Inside you can see:
- **Daily play time** — last 7 days as a bar chart
- **Learning scores** — per skill: Memory, Logic, Math, Language, Observation, Knowledge, Focus & Speed
- **Totals** — levels completed, stars, coins earned, current streak
- **Your child's name** — personalises Tigo's cheering

## How difficulty works
Levels 1–20 are Very Easy (age ~4–6) and ramp every 20 levels to Expert (age ~10–12). The **Tigo's Pick** card on the home screen gently recommends practice for whichever skill needs the most love — you'll see the same signal in the dashboard.

## Screen-time tips
- The daily bonus and streak reward *showing up*, not playing long.
- Quick challenges are 30 seconds — great for short sessions.

## Ads & Premium
Free version shows one short ad break after every 3 levels — never during a game. Premium (₹100, one-time) removes all ads forever, unlocks exclusive avatar items and boosts coin rewards.

## Privacy
No account is required to play. Everything stays on your device unless you create a parent account for cross-device backup. Children never have accounts of their own.
